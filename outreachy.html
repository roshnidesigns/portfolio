Context

The BlueGenes Redesign project is about redesigning the interface of InterMine Org, an open-source community focussed on integrating/operating biological data sources. As an Outreachy intern, I collaborated with the InterMine community to improve the usability and overall experience of this new platform for its launch- later the year.

Outreachy is an initiative that provides paid, remote internships to people subject to systemic bias and impacted by underrepresentation in tech. industry worldwide.
